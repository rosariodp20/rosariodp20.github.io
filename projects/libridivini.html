Bachelor’s Thesis

Development of web applications with ASP.NET Core – LibriDiVini

"LibridiVini" was developed as a Bachelor’s thesis project during my internship at Sautech Group. The application was created for a library in Puglia with the goal of introducing users to the world of wine. It was designed to be cross-platform, functioning on different devices regardless of operating system or screen size. "LibridiVini" encourages reading and learning through interactive multimedia content and aims to attract a broad audience, including younger users, by incorporating interactive games.

ER diagram of the LibridiVini database

The application is divided into two main sections: "Sorsi di Versi" and "Bianco e Nero". The first manages books, while the second handles multimedia content. "Sorsi di Versi" allows users to browse through available books and, upon selecting one, access information about the author, plot, context, and characters. It also enables users to leave a rating from 1 to 5 stars along with a review.

  • "Bianco e Nero" offers different categories from the main menu, featuring six distinct sections. "Il vino: una lunga storia" presents the history of wine from prehistoric times to the present.
  • "Appunti di viticoltura" illustrates the various stages of wine production.
  • "I vini del territorio" showcases the wines and wine-producing cities of Puglia.
  • "Gli studiosi del vino" and "Bere consapevole" provide educational videos, the first highlighting the unique characteristics of specific wines, and the second promoting responsible drinking habits.
  • "Giocare con il vino" features two interactive games: "Puzzle diVini", where users can solve wine-themed puzzles, and "RiConosci i Vini?", a game that challenges users to match wines with their regions of production.

Screen of the features of “Bianco e Nero”

Upon completion, all required features were successfully implemented, and the final application fully met the initial objectives. In terms of functional requirements, both "Sorsi di Versi" and "Bianco e Nero" were developed as planned. Compared to existing solutions on the market, which often offer only a superficial approach to the world of wine, "LibridiVini" provides a detailed and engaging experience, showcasing the diverse wines of Puglia while telling their stories and encouraging users to explore reading and multimedia content.

The entire project was developed using C# with ASP.NET Core and JavaScript.
