Profile

I am Rosario, a graduate of the University of Salerno with a Master's degree in Software Engineering and IT Management, earned with a final grade of 110 cum laude. I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. My choice to pursue this path was mainly motivated by the Software Engineering course I took during my Bachelor's, where I had the opportunity to learn about and observe the role of a Project Manager.

Regarding this aspect, I can confidently say that I have acquired solid knowledge both during my Bachelor's, thanks to the Software Engineering course, and during my Master's, through courses such as Software Engineering Management and Evolution, Software Dependability, and Software Project Management. Of course, I also consider programming an essential aspect of my education, which I have deepened through various projects and courses such as Programming 1, Object-Oriented Programming, Compilers, and more.

Throughout my studies, I have learned multiple programming languages, including Java, C, Python, JavaScript, Dart, as well as markup languages like HTML, CSS, and XML. Additionally, I have completed internships both in Italy as a Web Developer and in Spain as a Geodata Manager. I also gained work experience as an IT Consultant.

I can confidently say that programming is what I enjoy the most, with a particular passion for Python. In the near future, I would love to manage a project as a Project Manager or SCRUM Master, depending on the company's organizational dynamics.
